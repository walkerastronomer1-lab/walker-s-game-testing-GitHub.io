WIESSP Zombie Outbreak

First-person shooter prototype with a starter katana, six unlockable guns, armor upgrades, money from kills, and four buyable maps. Every katana kill splashes purple ooze.

Game Notes

Kill zombies to earn cash, buy better guns and armor, then unlock new maps. The starting katana takes about 2 hits to kill a zombie and splashes purple ooze on every close hit.

This prototype is designed to be clear, smooth, and easy on the eyes while showing unlock progression, money, and first-person action.
